Grace Nakiyemba

Grace Nakiyemba is a software engineering scholar at Groundbreaker Talents and an alumna of Code Queen. Raised in rural Uganda as the firstborn of seven children to a single mother, Grace’s journey into technology was shaped by resilience and determination. Once intimidated by computers, she discovered her passion for tech in high school and pursued it despite financial barriers and limited access. She is currently building her expertise in software development and cybersecurity, with a long-term vision of strengthening digital security in emerging communities. Grace is committed to expanding access to STEM opportunities for girls from underserved backgrounds and to creating visible pathways for those who believe technology is “not for them.” She believes access begins with the courage to say yes before feeling fully ready and her story reflects the power of persistence in turning fear into purpose.
